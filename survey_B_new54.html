Word Perception Study

Purpose

This study investigates how people associate the sounds of made-up words with certain qualities (e.g., size, weight, speed).

What will I do?

You will see pairs of made-up words and answer simple questions like "Which word feels bigger?" There are no right or wrong answers — we are interested in your intuitive responses.

  • Duration: approximately 5–10 minutes
  • 57 trials total (54 experimental + 3 attention checks)

Risks & Confidentiality

This study involves minimal risk. Your responses are anonymous. No personally identifiable information is collected. Data may be shared on OSF upon publication.

Voluntary Participation

Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time by closing this window.
