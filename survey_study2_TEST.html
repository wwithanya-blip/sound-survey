Sound Perception Study — Quick Test

Purpose

This study investigates how people associate the sounds of made-up words with certain qualities (e.g., weight, sharpness, speed).

What will I do?

You will listen to pairs of made-up words and answer simple questions like "Which word sounds heavier?" There are no right or wrong answers.

  • Duration: approximately 8–10 minutes
  • ~39 trials (36 experimental + attention checks)
  • Requires audio playback — please make sure your device can play sound
